About Me

I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Mathematics at Manav Rachna University, Faridabad, with over 15 years of experience in teaching and research.

My research focuses on Approximation Theory and Complex Polynomial Approximation, with particular interest in generalizations of established complex polynomial inequalities including Bernstein inequality, Malik, Ankeny and Rivlin, Aziz, and Xin Li inequalities.

I have published numerous papers in renowned international journals and presented my work at conferences worldwide. My Ph.D. thesis presented enhanced generalizations and extensions of several established complex polynomial inequalities.
